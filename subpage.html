About Me

Keith-Allen is a talented and dynamic performer with a passion for various types of dance such as Contemporary, Jazz, Ballet, Modern, Vogue, Tap, Hip-Hop and more. He is especially passionate about choreographing and being a choreographer. With five years of experience, he has captivated audiences with the fluidity, dynamics, and emotion he brings to both his dancing and choreography.
